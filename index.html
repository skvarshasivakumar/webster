Rule set 1

1.Ticket possession: Have a valid ticket or proof of purchase ready to show at all times.
2.Dress code: Wear appropriate clothing and footwear that is not offensive or dangerous.
3.Alcohol: In some venues, the consumption of alcohol may be restricted or prohibited.
4.Smoking: Smoking is usually not allowed inside the stadium.

Rule set 2

5.Food and drinks: Bringing outside food and drinks into the stadium is often prohibited.
6.Banners and flags: Banners and flags are usually allowed, but they must not be offensive or impede the view of other spectators.
7.Seating: Only sit in your designated seat and do not block the view of other spectators.
8.Behaviour: Refrain from disruptive behaviour, including shouting and cheering.

Rule set 3

9.Safety: Follow the instructions of stadium staff and emergency personnel in case of an emergency.
10.Respect: Show respect to players, officials, and other spectators at all times.
